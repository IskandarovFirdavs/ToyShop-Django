Our Story

Firdavs O'yinchoqlar Do'koni was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to bring joy to children through high-quality, educational toys that inspire creativity and learning.

What started as a small shop in Tashkent has grown into a beloved toy store that serves families throughout Uzbekistan. Our founder, Firdavs Karimov, believed that play is an essential part of childhood development, and this philosophy continues to guide everything we do.

Today, we carefully curate our selection of toys to ensure they meet our high standards for quality, safety, and educational value. We take pride in offering products that not only entertain but also help children learn and grow.

Our Mission

At Firdavs O'yinchoqlar Do'koni, our mission is to provide children with toys that spark imagination, foster learning, and create lasting memories. We believe that the right toys can make a significant impact on a child's development and happiness.
