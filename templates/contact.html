Frequently Asked Questions

What are your shipping options?

We offer standard shipping (3-5 business days) and express shipping (1-2 business days) throughout Uzbekistan. Free shipping is available for orders over $50.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unused and in their original packaging. Please contact our customer service team to initiate a return.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes, we offer gift wrapping services for a small additional fee. You can select this option during checkout and even include a personalized message.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package on our website or through the shipping carrier's site.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we only ship within Uzbekistan. We hope to expand our shipping options to neighboring countries in the near future.
